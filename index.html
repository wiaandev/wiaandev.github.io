SerenGuard Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09 November 2023

Thank you for choosing SerenGuard, an app designed to foster a strong connection between officers and their communities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information in the course of using the SerenGuard app.

1. Information we collect

  • User Profile Information: We collect basic user information, including name, email address, and contact details
  • Reports and Images: Officers may submit reports, including images, related to incidents in the community.

2. How we use your information

  • Community Engagement: Your information is used to facilitate communication and engagement between officers and the community
  • Incident Reports: The app utilizes AI to assist in labeling images from incident reports.

3. Information Sharing

  • Officers and Community: Information submitted by officers is shared with the community to keep them informed about incidents in the area.
  • Third-Party Services: We may use third-party services to enhance app functionality.

4. Map functionality

  • Location Data: The app uses location data to display incident reports on the map. Civilians are informed to stay away from marked locations.

5. Security measures

  • We implement security measures to protect your personal information and the confidentiality of incident reports.

6. Users rights

  • Users have the right to access their personal information.

7. Children's privacy

  • The SerenGuard app is intended for use by any individuals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app's features or to comply with legal requirements. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
